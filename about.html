Welcome to Career Katta - Your Ultimate Career Companion

Career Katta is a mobile app that's designed to support your educational and career needs. Our key features ensure you have the best tools and resources to succeed in your learning journey.

Key Features:

  • Live Classes: Participate in real-time interactive classes to enhance your learning journey. Our live classes cover a wide range of subjects, ensuring that you receive top-notch education.
  • Recorded Classes: Can't make it to a live session? No problem! Access our library of recorded classes, so you never miss out on valuable content.
  • Notifications: Stay up to date with important class schedules, quiz and exam announcements, assignment deadlines, and certification updates. Never miss a critical event in your educational path.
  • Personalized Learning: We believe in tailoring your experience to meet your unique academic needs. Whether you are a student or a professional, our app provides personalized recommendations to help you succeed.
