Refund Policy

Thank you for using Career Katta. We value your business and want to ensure you are completely satisfied with your purchase. Please read our refund policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations when seeking a refund for our services.

Once payment is made it cannot be refunded under any circumstances.


If a amount was deducted from your account but it is not reflected on the app the amount will be refunded within 3 working days. The refund will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase.

To request a refund, please follow these steps:
Contact Support: Email our support team at careerkatta365@gmail.com with your refund request and a detailed explanation of the issue.
Provide Information: Include your name, email address, date of purchase, and any relevant details about the problem you encountered.
Verification: Our support team will review your request and may ask for additional information to verify your identity and the transaction.
Refund Decision: We will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund request via email.
