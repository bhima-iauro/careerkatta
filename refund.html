Refund Policy

Thank you for using Career Katta. We value your business and want to ensure you are completely satisfied with your purchase. Please read our refund policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations when seeking a refund for our services.

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following circumstances:
Technical Issues: If you experience technical issues that prevent you from using our app as intended, and our support team is unable to resolve the problem within a reasonable time frame.
Unauthorized Purchases: If there are unauthorized purchases made through your account, please report them to us within 24 hours of the transaction date.

2. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:
Contact Support: Email our support team at uvajagar@gmail.com with your refund request and a detailed explanation of the issue.
Provide Information: Include your name, email address, date of purchase, and any relevant details about the problem you encountered.
Verification: Our support team will review your request and may ask for additional information to verify your identity and the transaction.
Refund Decision: We will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund request via email.

3. Refund Timeline

Refunds will be processed within 3 days of approval. The refund will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase.
